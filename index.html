Who We Are

Originally starting as a Cleaning Company, we soon discovered the needs of you, the client, who’s always striving to find a trustworthy and dependable Partner who, not only, will accept a unique task but will accomplish said task on time, at the price you were given.

From the first time we had been asked could we do wood backing— where another trade had fallen— we saw the need for Reliability, Credibility and Honesty.
This demand for Old School Values has driven our growth; and over 6 months ago we changed our name to Brillo Construction Services (DBA) and have organized into four Divisions to help cater to your needs.
